Evolution of Adobe Animate

  • First developed as FutureSplash Animator in May 1996.
  • Rebranded as Macromedia Flash in December 1996.
  • Rebranded to Adobe Flash Professional as a way to distinguish it from the Player in 2005.
  • Rebranded as Adobe Animate on December 1, 2015, to completely disassociate it from the Adobe Flash Player.
  • Logo completely redesigned for the very first time in 20 years on June 16, 2020, as part of Adobe's 'Evolving Brand Identity'.

Name of Milestone Info About Milestone Date of Milestone
